Boardwalk

Boardwalk product no longer available - Please contact Vigil Antislip for information on our alternative product range.

Strong, immovable and impenetrable, Vigil Antislip Boardwalk has been specifically formulated for application to flooring areas where an increased coefficient of structural integrity is required to create a high performance non-slip arena.

Originally developed for false flooring in static sensitive control rooms, the 10mm thick foundation is constructed of phenolic resin and saw dust cured under extreme heat and pressure creating a very dense, structurally sound product.

Boardwalk is completely corrosion resistant and impervious to chemical spills and temperature extremes. It exhibits excellent non-conductive properties for additional safety in environments exposed to volatile gasses or where sensitive computer or control equipment is in use.

  1. Dimensions

    Vigil Antislip Boardwalk is measured in two dimensions; length ways (A) and width (B). The thickness ranges from 11mm - 13mm dependant upon the grade required.

    A:Length of Boardwalk: There are two standard lengths; 600mm and 200mm. unlike Metal Deck, due to the greater thickness of Boardwalk, each sheet should be butted up against the next to avoid the possibility of trips occuring.

    B:Width of Boardwalk: As with Metal Deck, Boardwalk need not cover the entire width of the walkway or ramp. Personnel tend not to walk on the edges of walkways so it is recommended that Boardwalk 150mm - 300mm narrower than the overall walkway be ordered, ie. if ordering Boardwalk for an 800mm wide walkway, 600mm wide Boardwalk will provide ample protection.
